# Sarung BHS

Indonesia's beloved sarong brand doubled online sales in just two months by embracing Google Ads to reach a new generation of buyers.

## The Challenge

Sarung BHS is one of Indonesia's most recognized sarong brands, with decades of heritage in producing traditional woven garments worn by millions of Indonesians for prayer, ceremonies, and daily life. While the brand had strong offline distribution through traditional markets and retail stores, its online presence was minimal. As younger Indonesian consumers increasingly turned to e-commerce and digital channels for shopping, BHS risked losing relevance with a critical demographic.

The challenge was twofold: first, drive immediate online sales through digital advertising; second, position a heritage brand as accessible and appealing to digital-native consumers without diluting its cultural significance. BHS had limited experience with digital marketing, and the team needed to build capability quickly.

## The Solution

Sarung BHS launched Google Ads campaigns focusing on Search and Display to drive traffic to their online store. The strategy was designed for rapid impact — getting measurable results within the first two months to prove the digital channel's viability.

<div class="callout callout-info">

**Technical Approach:** Search campaigns targeted both branded terms ("sarung BHS") and category terms ("beli sarung online," "sarung premium"). Display campaigns used affinity and in-market audiences targeting Indonesian men interested in fashion, religious apparel, and cultural products. Remarketing lists captured website visitors who browsed product pages but did not purchase, serving them dynamic ads featuring the exact products they had viewed.

</div>

The team created landing pages optimized for mobile — recognizing that the majority of Indonesian internet users access the web via smartphones. Product pages featured high-quality imagery, size guides, and trust signals like authenticity guarantees and easy return policies.

Campaign timing was aligned with religious calendar events, particularly the lead-up to Ramadan and Eid al-Fitr, when sarong purchases spike dramatically. BHS increased budgets and bids during these high-demand windows to capture seasonal intent.

Doubling online sales in just two months validated digital advertising as a growth channel for Sarung BHS. The speed of results was particularly important for a traditional brand entering the digital space — it provided the internal confidence needed to sustain and grow investment in Google Ads. The campaign also demonstrated that heritage brands can successfully bridge traditional and digital retail without compromising their identity.

## Key Takeaway

<div class="callout callout-tip">

**For heritage and traditional brands going digital:** Start with Search to capture existing demand for your brand and category, then layer in Display for reach. Align campaigns with cultural and religious moments when your product is most relevant. Mobile optimization is non-negotiable in Southeast Asian markets — ensure your landing pages load fast and convert well on smartphones.
