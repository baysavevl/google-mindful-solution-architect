# GoGa

Vietnam's growing service platform deployed Performance Max campaigns to drive qualified leads at a lower cost across all Google channels.

## The Challenge

GoGa operates as a multi-service platform connecting Vietnamese consumers with providers for cleaning, repair, beauty, and other home and lifestyle services. As the platform scaled, lead generation became increasingly expensive. The company ran separate campaigns across Search, Display, and YouTube, each with its own targeting and bidding strategy — leading to fragmented performance data and inefficient budget allocation.

The core challenge was unifying their advertising approach to reach potential customers across the entire Google ecosystem while reducing the cost per qualified lead. With multiple service verticals and customer segments, GoGa needed a solution that could dynamically allocate budget to the best-performing combinations of audience, creative, and channel.

## The Solution

GoGa adopted Performance Max, Google's AI-driven campaign type that serves ads across Search, Display, YouTube, Gmail, Discover, and Maps from a single campaign. This allowed GoGa to consolidate their fragmented campaign structure into a unified, goal-oriented approach.

<div class="callout callout-info">

**Technical Approach:** GoGa created asset groups organized by service vertical — home cleaning, appliance repair, beauty services — each with tailored headlines, descriptions, images, and video assets. Audience signals were configured using first-party data from existing customers and custom segments based on in-market and affinity audiences relevant to each service category.

</div>

The team set up offline conversion tracking, feeding lead quality data back into the campaign to help Google's algorithm distinguish between leads that converted into paying customers and those that did not. This feedback loop was critical for optimizing toward business outcomes rather than just form submissions.

GoGa also leveraged the URL expansion feature within Performance Max, allowing Google to match relevant search queries to the most appropriate landing pages on their website — even queries the team had not explicitly targeted. This expanded their reach into long-tail searches they would have otherwise missed.

By consolidating campaigns into Performance Max, the marketing team also reduced operational overhead — spending less time on manual campaign management and more time on creative strategy and customer experience improvements.

## Key Takeaway

<div class="callout callout-tip">

**For lead-gen platforms:** Performance Max works best when you feed it quality signals. Set up offline conversion tracking to tell the algorithm which leads actually become customers. Organize asset groups by product or service line, and use first-party audience signals to guide — not restrict — the AI's targeting. The 20% CPL improvement GoGa achieved came from letting the system optimize across all channels simultaneously.
