# Ru9 — The Sleep Company

Vietnam's first bed-in-a-box brand used Google Search Ads to turn online mattress shopping into a high-performance growth channel.

## The Challenge

When Ru9 launched in 2019, the Vietnamese mattress market was dominated by traditional brick-and-mortar retailers. Consumers were accustomed to testing mattresses in physical stores before purchasing, making the direct-to-consumer online model a tough sell. Brand awareness was minimal, and convincing Vietnamese shoppers to spend millions of VND on a mattress they had never physically tried was the company's primary hurdle.

Additionally, Ru9 faced stiff competition from established bedding brands with deep distribution networks and large marketing budgets. As a startup, Ru9 needed a cost-efficient acquisition channel that could reach high-intent buyers — people actively searching for better sleep solutions — without burning through limited venture capital.

## The Solution

Ru9 turned to Google Search Ads to capture demand at the exact moment consumers were looking for mattress solutions online. The team built tightly themed ad groups around high-intent keywords such as "mua nem online" (buy mattress online), "nem foam," and branded competitor terms to intercept shoppers during their research phase.

<div class="callout callout-info">

**Technical Approach:** Ru9 structured campaigns around three keyword tiers: branded terms for retention, generic high-intent terms for acquisition, and competitor terms for conquest. Each tier had dedicated ad copy and landing pages optimized for conversion, with unique selling points like the 100-night trial prominently featured.

</div>

The team complemented their keyword strategy with ad extensions — sitelinks to product pages, callout extensions highlighting free delivery and trial periods, and structured snippets showcasing mattress types. This approach maximized ad real estate on the search results page and boosted click-through rates significantly.

Ru9 also implemented conversion tracking tied directly to completed purchases, enabling the Google Ads algorithm to optimize bids toward users most likely to buy. This data-driven approach allowed the team to shift budget toward the highest-performing keywords and audiences in near real-time.

The 900% ROI meant that for every dollar Ru9 invested in Google Search Ads, the company generated nine dollars in revenue. This exceptional return validated the D2C model in a market where online mattress shopping was virtually non-existent just a few years earlier. The efficiency of Search Ads allowed Ru9 to reinvest profits into product development and expand their sleep product lineup.

## Key Takeaway

<div class="callout callout-tip">

**For D2C brands in emerging markets:** Google Search Ads let you capture demand precisely when consumers are ready to buy. Structure campaigns around intent tiers — branded, generic, and competitor — and pair them with strong landing pages that address trust barriers like free trials and easy returns. In markets transitioning to e-commerce, being the first to own high-intent search terms can deliver outsized returns.
