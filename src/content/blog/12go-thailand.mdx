# 12Go

Thailand-based online travel platform 12Go leveraged AI-powered bidding in Google Search to maximize conversion value and scale bookings across Southeast Asia.

## The Challenge

12Go is an online travel platform that aggregates and sells transportation tickets — buses, trains, ferries, and flights — across Southeast Asia. With routes spanning Thailand, Vietnam, Cambodia, Malaysia, and beyond, the company operates in an incredibly fragmented market. Ticket prices range from a few dollars for a local bus to hundreds for premium train routes, making it difficult to apply a one-size-fits-all bidding strategy.

The team's manual bidding approach could not efficiently account for the vast differences in booking values across routes, seasons, and traveler segments. High-value bookings like international train routes were being bid on the same basis as low-margin local bus tickets. The company needed a smarter way to allocate ad spend toward the bookings that generated the most revenue.

## The Solution

12Go implemented Google's AI-powered Smart Bidding strategies — specifically Maximize Conversion Value with target ROAS — across their Search campaigns. This allowed the algorithm to evaluate each auction individually and bid higher for queries likely to result in high-value bookings.

<div class="callout callout-info">

**Technical Approach:** 12Go fed transaction-level revenue data back to Google Ads via conversion tracking, assigning the actual booking value to each conversion. This enabled the Maximize Conversion Value bidding strategy to differentiate between a $5 bus ticket and a $200 sleeper train booking. The algorithm used this data alongside real-time signals — user location, device, time of day, search query — to set optimal bids for each auction.

</div>

Campaign structure was organized by transportation mode and corridor (e.g., Bangkok-to-Chiang Mai trains, Hanoi-to-Sapa buses), allowing the team to set appropriate ROAS targets for each segment. For high-value corridors, they set more aggressive ROAS targets, while lower-value routes had relaxed targets to maintain volume.

12Go also invested in Broad Match keywords paired with Smart Bidding, trusting the algorithm to identify relevant queries beyond their manually curated keyword lists. This combination unlocked long-tail queries from travelers searching in multiple languages and with diverse phrasings.

## Key Takeaway

<div class="callout callout-tip">

**For travel and marketplace businesses:** If your products or services have widely varying values, use Maximize Conversion Value bidding with transaction-level revenue data. The AI can differentiate between high and low-value conversions in ways that manual bidding never could. Pair it with Broad Match to expand reach, and structure campaigns by product segment to maintain appropriate ROAS targets.
