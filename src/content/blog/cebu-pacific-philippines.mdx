# Cebu Pacific Air

The Philippines' largest budget carrier leveraged Display & Video 360 to deliver personalized ads to international travelers, achieving a 7X return on ad spend.

## The Challenge

Cebu Pacific Air is the Philippines' leading low-cost carrier, operating an extensive domestic and international network. As the airline expanded its international routes — particularly across Asia-Pacific and the Middle East (serving the large Filipino diaspora) — it needed to reach potential travelers in markets where brand awareness was lower compared to the domestic Philippines market.

Traditional display advertising delivered broad reach but poor efficiency. Generic banner ads shown to all users regardless of their travel interests resulted in low click-through rates and minimal bookings. Cebu Pacific needed a way to serve the right message to the right traveler at the right time — matching specific route promotions to travelers most likely to book those routes.

## The Solution

Cebu Pacific adopted Google's Display & Video 360 (DV360) platform to execute a programmatic advertising strategy built around personalization and audience precision.

<div class="callout callout-info">

**Technical Approach:** Using DV360's data-driven creative capabilities, Cebu Pacific built dynamic ad templates that automatically populated with route-specific information — destination imagery, fare prices, and departure cities — based on the viewer's location and browsing behavior. A user in Dubai browsing travel content would see ads for Manila flights with current promotional fares, while a user in Singapore might see Cebu beach destination creative.

</div>

The team created custom audience segments combining first-party data (website visitors who searched for specific routes but did not book) with third-party travel intent signals available through DV360. This layered targeting ensured ads reached people actively planning travel rather than broad demographic groups.

Frequency capping and sequential messaging were implemented to guide users through the booking funnel. Initial impressions focused on destination inspiration, followed by fare-specific retargeting for users who had visited route pages, and finally urgency-driven messaging ("limited seats") for users who had begun but not completed bookings.

The 7X ROAS represented a dramatic improvement over Cebu Pacific's previous display advertising performance. Personalization was the decisive factor — showing travelers ads for routes relevant to their location and interests fundamentally changed the efficiency equation. The DV360 approach allowed Cebu Pacific to scale international marketing without proportionally scaling creative production costs, since dynamic templates automatically adapted to each route and market.

## Key Takeaway

<div class="callout callout-tip">

**For airlines targeting international travelers:** Display & Video 360's dynamic creative capabilities let you personalize at scale without producing thousands of individual ad variations. Build templates that pull in route, fare, and destination data dynamically. Layer first-party website data with travel intent signals for precise targeting, and use sequential messaging to move travelers from inspiration to booking.
