# The One Digi Corp

A Vietnamese visa services company leveraged Google's AI-powered Search features to double revenue and dramatically increase conversions across international markets.

## The Challenge

The One Digi Corp provides visa processing and immigration services for travelers visiting Vietnam. The company's customer base is inherently international — spanning dozens of nationalities, each with distinct visa requirements, search behaviors, and languages. Scaling ad campaigns manually across this fragmented audience was both time-intensive and inefficient.

The team had historically relied on exact match and phrase match keywords, which limited their ability to capture the wide variety of search queries potential customers use when researching Vietnamese visa options. Terms vary drastically by language, intent level, and specificity — from "Vietnam e-visa application" to "do I need a visa for Ho Chi Minh City." The company was leaving significant demand on the table.

## The Solution

The One Digi Corp shifted to a Broad Match keyword strategy combined with Target CPA bidding, entrusting Google's AI to match relevant queries and set optimal bids in real time. This was a deliberate move from manual control to AI-powered optimization.

<div class="callout callout-info">

**Technical Approach:** By switching from restrictive match types to Broad Match, the team allowed Google's algorithm to interpret user intent beyond literal keyword matches. Combined with Target CPA bidding, the system automatically adjusted bids for each auction based on signals like device, location, time of day, and user intent — ensuring The One Digi Corp competed aggressively only when conversion probability was high.

</div>

The team organized campaigns by visa type and target nationality, ensuring ad copy and landing pages aligned with specific traveler needs. They implemented comprehensive conversion tracking to give the bidding algorithm clean data, distinguishing between visa inquiries, form submissions, and completed applications.

A key part of the strategy was trusting the AI with sufficient budget headroom and learning periods. Rather than making frequent manual adjustments, the team allowed campaigns to accumulate data and optimize over multi-week cycles, which proved essential for the algorithm to reach peak performance.
