# Tri Petch Isuzu Sales

Thailand's official Isuzu distributor used Google Search Ads to capture automotive purchase intent and increase qualified leads by 67%.

## The Challenge

Tri Petch Isuzu Sales is the authorized distributor of Isuzu vehicles in Thailand, a market where pickup trucks are not just commercial vehicles but a way of life. The Isuzu D-Max is consistently among the top-selling vehicles in the country. However, as consumer research behavior shifted online, Tri Petch recognized that potential buyers were doing extensive research on Google before ever visiting a dealership.

The company needed to intercept these high-intent shoppers during their digital research phase and convert them into qualified leads for their dealer network. Traditional media — TV, print, and outdoor — drove brand awareness but lacked the precision to capture demand at the moment of intent. Tri Petch needed a measurable channel that connected online research to offline showroom visits and test drives.

## The Solution

Tri Petch Isuzu Sales implemented a comprehensive Google Search Ads strategy targeting the full automotive purchase funnel — from early research queries to ready-to-buy intent signals.

<div class="callout callout-info">

**Technical Approach:** The campaign structure followed the automotive buyer journey: top-funnel campaigns targeting category terms like "รถกระบะ" (pickup truck) and "รถยนต์ใหม่" (new car), mid-funnel campaigns targeting model-specific terms like "Isuzu D-Max 2026 ราคา" (D-Max 2026 price), and bottom-funnel campaigns targeting action-oriented terms like "จองรถ Isuzu" (book Isuzu) and dealer location queries.

</div>

Each funnel stage had dedicated landing pages — research-stage ads led to model comparison and feature pages, while high-intent ads drove to lead forms for test drive bookings and dealer contact requests. The team utilized location extensions to show the nearest dealer and call extensions for immediate phone inquiries.

Tri Petch also implemented store visit conversions where possible, connecting online ad clicks to physical dealership visits using aggregated, anonymized location data. This gave the team visibility into the true ROI of their Search campaigns beyond online form submissions.

## Key Takeaway

<div class="callout callout-tip">

**For automotive brands:** Structure Search campaigns around the buyer journey — from category research to model comparison to purchase action. Use location and call extensions to bridge online intent with offline dealership visits. In markets like Thailand where vehicle purchases still happen at dealerships, the role of Search is to own the research moment and guide buyers to your showroom.
