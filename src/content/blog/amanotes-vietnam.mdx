# Amanotes

Vietnam's largest indie mobile game publisher harnessed Google App Campaigns to drive efficient user acquisition across global markets.

## The Challenge

Amanotes had already established itself as a leader in music-based mobile gaming with titles like Magic Tiles 3 and Tiles Hop amassing hundreds of millions of downloads worldwide. However, as the hyper-casual gaming market became increasingly crowded, the cost of acquiring new users climbed steadily. The company needed to scale installs across diverse global markets — from Southeast Asia to North America — while keeping cost per install (CPI) sustainable.

The challenge was compounded by the need to acquire users who would not only install the game but also engage and monetize through in-app ads and purchases. Low-quality installs that churned quickly would erode the economics of the entire portfolio.

## The Solution

Amanotes adopted Google App Campaigns to automate and optimize user acquisition at scale. Unlike traditional ad campaigns that require manual creative and placement management, App Campaigns use machine learning to distribute ads across Google Search, Google Play, YouTube, and the Google Display Network — automatically finding the best-performing combinations.

<div class="callout callout-info">

**Technical Approach:** Amanotes provided a diverse set of creative assets — gameplay videos, screenshots, and short text lines — and let Google's ML models test thousands of combinations to identify top performers across different markets and placements. Campaigns were optimized toward in-app actions (target CPA) rather than just installs, ensuring higher user quality.

</div>

The team segmented campaigns by game title and geographic region, allowing budget to flow toward the highest-LTV (lifetime value) markets. For established titles, they focused on re-engagement campaigns to bring lapsed players back, while newer titles received install-optimized campaigns with broader targeting.

Amanotes also invested in creative refresh cycles, regularly updating video assets and ad copy to combat creative fatigue — a common issue in high-volume mobile gaming campaigns where the same audiences see ads repeatedly.

Google App Campaigns became the backbone of their global user acquisition strategy.

## Key Takeaway

<div class="callout callout-tip">

**For mobile app publishers:** Optimize App Campaigns toward in-app actions rather than raw installs. Feed the algorithm diverse, high-quality creative assets and refresh them regularly to combat fatigue. Segmenting by geography and game title gives you granular control over budget allocation while still benefiting from Google's ML-driven optimization.
