## The Challenge

Klook is a leading travel activities and experiences platform serving Asia-Pacific, offering everything from theme park tickets and guided tours to airport transfers and local food experiences. With thousands of activities across dozens of cities, Klook's marketing challenge was matching the right experience to the right traveler at the right moment in their planning journey.

Travel experience purchases differ from flights and hotels — they're often booked closer to the travel date, highly influenced by visual inspiration, and span a vast range of price points from $10 walking tours to $500 multi-day excursions. Klook needed a strategy that could handle this complexity while scaling across APAC markets with diverse languages, travel preferences, and booking behaviors.

## The Solution

Klook deployed AI Max campaigns integrated with YouTube advertising to create a full-funnel approach that combined AI-powered search with visual storytelling.

<div class="callout callout-info">

**Technical Approach:** AI Max campaigns for Search used Google's most advanced AI to automatically optimize keyword matching, ad creative, and bidding across Klook's massive inventory of travel experiences. The system dynamically matched search queries to the most relevant experience pages, even for long-tail queries like "best cooking class in Bangkok for families" or "Osaka day trips from Tokyo." YouTube campaigns served visually rich video content showcasing experiences, targeting users based on travel intent signals and upcoming trip indicators.

</div>

The integration between AI Max Search and YouTube campaigns was strategic. YouTube drove top-of-funnel awareness and inspiration — showing travelers what experiences were possible at their destination. AI Max Search then captured the downstream demand when those same travelers searched for specific activities. Attribution modeling connected YouTube views to subsequent search conversions, giving Klook visibility into the full-funnel impact.

Klook fed granular conversion value data into the campaigns, assigning actual booking revenue to each conversion. For high-value experiences (premium tours, multi-day packages), the algorithm learned to bid more aggressively. The team also used audience signals from their loyalty program and first-party booking data to guide AI Max's targeting toward high-LTV customer profiles.

The combination of AI Max and YouTube proved that visual inspiration and intent capture work as a unified system rather than isolated channels. Travelers who saw YouTube content featuring destination experiences and later searched for those experiences converted at significantly higher rates and booked higher-value packages than those reached through search alone.

## Key Takeaway

<div class="callout callout-tip">
