# Kilang Besi Choong Len

A Malaysian steel fabrication company achieved 3,000% ROI using Google Search Ads, transforming a RM10,000 investment into RM300,000 in B2B contracts.

## The Challenge

Kilang Besi Choong Len is a steel fabrication and manufacturing company based in Malaysia, serving the construction, infrastructure, and industrial sectors. Like many traditional B2B manufacturers in Southeast Asia, the company relied heavily on word-of-mouth referrals, trade shows, and personal relationships to generate new business. While these channels produced steady revenue, growth was slow and unpredictable.

The company faced a generational shift in how procurement decisions were being made. Younger project managers and procurement officers were increasingly searching Google for steel suppliers rather than relying solely on established networks. Kilang Besi was invisible in these digital searches, ceding ground to competitors who had adopted online marketing. With a modest marketing budget, the company needed a channel that could deliver measurable, high-value returns.

## The Solution

Kilang Besi Choong Len launched Google Search Ads campaigns with a focused RM10,000 budget, targeting high-intent B2B search queries related to steel fabrication, structural steel supply, and custom metalwork in Malaysia.

<div class="callout callout-info">

**Technical Approach:** The campaign strategy was hyper-focused on bottom-funnel, high-commercial-intent keywords: "steel fabrication Malaysia," "kilang besi" (steel factory), "structural steel supplier KL," and specific product terms like "mild steel plate supplier" and "I-beam fabrication." Geographic targeting was set to Peninsular Malaysia, with bid adjustments increasing for the Klang Valley industrial corridor where construction activity — and thus demand — was highest.

</div>

Landing pages were designed specifically for B2B inquiries, featuring the company's capabilities, machinery specifications, certifications, past projects with photos, and prominent contact forms and WhatsApp click-to-chat buttons. The team recognized that in Malaysian B2B, buyers want to verify capability and credibility before making contact.

Conversion tracking was set up for form submissions, phone calls, and WhatsApp clicks. Given the high average order value in steel fabrication (individual contracts worth tens or hundreds of thousands of ringgit), even a small number of conversions could deliver outsized revenue. The team focused on lead quality over lead volume, using negative keywords aggressively to filter out irrelevant searches.

## Key Takeaway

<div class="callout callout-tip">

**For B2B manufacturers with high-value contracts:** Google Search Ads can deliver extraordinary ROI when your average deal size is large. Focus on bottom-funnel, high-intent keywords and prioritize lead quality over volume. In Southeast Asian B2B markets, ensure landing pages include WhatsApp contact options and showcase certifications, past projects, and production capabilities. A modest ad budget can yield massive returns when each lead is worth five or six figures.
