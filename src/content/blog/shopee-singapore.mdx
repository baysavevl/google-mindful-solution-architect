# Shopee

Southeast Asia's leading e-commerce marketplace used AI Max and Google Shopping campaigns to double order volume and increase ROI by 49%.

## The Challenge

Shopee is one of Southeast Asia's largest e-commerce platforms, operating across Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam, the Philippines, and beyond. With millions of product listings from hundreds of thousands of sellers, managing Google Shopping campaigns at this scale was a formidable challenge. Product feeds needed to be accurate, up-to-date, and optimized across multiple countries with different languages, currencies, and consumer behaviors.

The platform faced intense competition from other e-commerce marketplaces for Shopping ad real estate. With similar products available across competing platforms, the ability to surface the most relevant product listings with competitive pricing and compelling imagery was critical. Traditional Shopping campaign management could not keep pace with the dynamic nature of Shopee's massive, multi-market inventory.

## The Solution

Shopee adopted AI Max campaigns for Shopping, leveraging Google's most advanced AI-powered campaign management to automate bidding, targeting, and creative optimization across their enormous product catalog.

<div class="callout callout-info">

**Technical Approach:** Shopee invested heavily in product feed optimization — ensuring titles, descriptions, images, pricing, and availability were accurate and enriched with relevant attributes. Feed management tools were used to transform raw seller data into Google-optimized product listings at scale. AI Max campaigns then used this enriched feed data combined with machine learning to determine optimal bids, placements, and product groupings for each auction.

</div>

The team structured campaigns by product category and market, allowing AI Max to optimize within defined segments while maintaining budget control. Custom labels in the product feed tagged items by margin tier, popularity, and promotional status, giving the algorithm additional signals for bid optimization.

For cross-market campaigns, Shopee implemented localized feeds for each country — adapting product titles, descriptions, and pricing to local languages and currencies. This localization effort ensured that Shopping ads appeared natural and relevant to consumers in each market rather than showing generic, one-size-fits-all listings.

Shopee's investment in feed quality gave the AI better raw material to work with, amplifying the impact of automated optimization.

## Key Takeaway

<div class="callout callout-tip">

**For e-commerce marketplaces:** AI Max Shopping campaigns are only as good as the product feed data powering them. Invest in feed optimization — accurate titles, high-quality images, competitive pricing, and rich product attributes. Use custom labels to signal margin, popularity, and promotional status to the algorithm. In multi-market operations, localize feeds fully rather than relying on auto-translation.
