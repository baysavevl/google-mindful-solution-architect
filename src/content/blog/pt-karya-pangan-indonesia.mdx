# PT Karya Pangan Sejahtera

An Indonesian food manufacturer used Google Search Ads to grow website traffic by 6X and double their customer base.

<span class="badge">Indonesia</span> <span class="badge">Food Manufacturing</span> <span class="badge">Search Ads</span>

## The Challenge

PT Karya Pangan Sejahtera is an Indonesian food manufacturing company producing snacks, seasonings, and packaged food products. While the company had a solid B2B distribution network supplying retailers and wholesalers, they recognized an opportunity to grow their B2B pipeline and explore direct-to-consumer channels through digital marketing.

The problem was visibility. When food retailers, distributors, or even consumers searched for products online, PT Karya Pangan was virtually invisible. Competitors with stronger digital presences were capturing the growing wave of B2B buyers using Google to research suppliers and products. The company's website existed but received minimal organic traffic, and they had no experience with paid digital advertising.

## The Solution

PT Karya Pangan Sejahtera launched Google Search Ads campaigns to put their products and brand in front of buyers actively searching for food products and suppliers in Indonesia.

<div class="callout callout-info">

**Technical Approach:** Campaigns were structured around two audiences: B2B buyers (distributors, retailers, resellers) and end consumers. B2B campaigns targeted terms like "supplier makanan ringan" (snack food supplier), "distributor bumbu" (seasoning distributor), and specific product category keywords. Consumer campaigns targeted product-level terms. Each audience segment had dedicated landing pages with relevant CTAs — wholesale inquiry forms for B2B and product catalogs for consumers.

</div>

The company invested in proper conversion tracking from the start, measuring form submissions, phone calls, and WhatsApp clicks — the last being particularly important in Indonesia where business inquiries frequently happen over WhatsApp. This gave the team clear visibility into which keywords and campaigns drove real business outcomes.

Ad copy emphasized key trust factors for B2B buyers: BPOM certification (Indonesia's food safety authority), production capacity, minimum order quantities, and years of manufacturing experience. For consumer audiences, ads highlighted product variety, taste, and local availability.

The 6X traffic increase and doubling of the customer base represented a transformation for PT Karya Pangan Sejahtera. For a traditional food manufacturer, this level of digital growth was unprecedented and opened up new business relationships that would have been difficult to establish through conventional sales channels alone. Search Ads became the company's most efficient lead generation tool, outperforming trade shows and cold outreach on a cost-per-customer basis.

## Key Takeaway

<div class="callout callout-tip">

**For B2B manufacturers entering digital marketing:** Google Search Ads are one of the fastest ways to become visible to buyers actively looking for your products. Structure campaigns around both B2B buyer intent and consumer demand. In markets like Indonesia, track WhatsApp clicks as conversions — it's where business happens. Emphasize certifications and trust signals in ad copy to convert research-stage visitors into qualified leads.
